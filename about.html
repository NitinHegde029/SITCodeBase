Evolution of the Association

ECO Club established on 01-08-2018 at Siddaganga Institute of Technology, Tumakuru is a voluntary group comprised primarily of faculty and students, with the aim of continuously promoting environmental awareness within the campus and the larger community.
This ECO Club is functioning under the Department of Information Science & Engineering, Siddaganga Institute of Technology, Tumakuru. ECO Club engages continuously to understand challenges to our environment, eco system and biodiversity.
ECO club impart environmental education among young minds and prepare them accepts and tackle the challenges of climate change and environmental sustainability through innovative ideas and academic research, transforming that into actions on campus and beyond. Eco club promotes the concept of stewardship.
ECO Club connects academia and realizes the roles of academia to accept environmental challenges and work towards solutions. ECO club collaborate with state and National initiatives and schemes towards promotion of our environment.

Mission


Our mission is to improve the environmental quality of our community
by partnering with area businesses, community leaders and neighbours to work together
to create a clean and safe place to live and work for future generations.


Vision


To create a healthier, happier and more fertile community where staff and
students acquire environmental education and transformed into social well beings of national importance,
excited about their work and proud of their achievements.


Goals


  • To create a community that is aware of challenges of our environment and work towards development, preservation and maintenance of our environment in collaboration with various initiatives and schemes of national, state and local Government.
  • To Foster clear awareness of, and concern about economic, social, political and ecological interdependence in urban and rural areas.
  • To provide every person with opportunities to acquire the knowledge, values, attitudes, commitment and skills needed to protect and improve our environment.

Activities of the ECO Club


  • Organizing Tree Plantation programme
  • Organizing Seeds Collection Program and donate to Karnataka State Forest Nurseries.
  • Organizing Environmental Education Programmes.
  • Promote young minds to work in forest nurseries.
  • Development of Bio Diversity Parks, Gomala lands, Lake sides etc.
  • Collaborate with County’s ‘Jalashakti Abhiyan’ Scheme and to implement AICTE prescribed initiatives and schemes such as One Student One Tree, etc.
  • Identifying suitable dry lands and lakes and to undertake afforestation works.
  • Promote Nature Photography among young minds by organizing Nature Photography Competition.
  • Organizing Eco Tours
  • Identifying funding schemes of Govt. agencies & Submission of project proposals to funding agencies to undertake environmental activities.
  • Evaluation and assessment of Environmental parameters and Ecological systems.
  • Raising funds from donors and under various schemes to plan and execute environmental activities.
  • The Jal Shakti Abhiyan is aimed to accelerate water harvesting, conservation and bore well recharge activities in 256 water-stressed districts.
  • Hon’ble HRD Minister Dr. Ramesh Pokhriyal “Nishank” launched a campaign “One Student One Tree” on 20th July. This initiative is in line with the Hon’ble Prime Minister’s idea of a green and healthy environment.

Eco Club's Promise


 
       As a member of ECO Club, SIT campus, Tumakuru, I promise
  • To attend club meetings regularly
  • To prepare all of my speech and leadership projects to the best of my ability, basing them on projects in the Competent Communication, Advanced Communication or Competent Leadership manuals.
  • To prepare for and fulfil meeting assignments.
  • To provide fellow members with helpful, constructive evaluations.
  • To help the club maintain the positive, friendly environment necessary for all members to learn and grow.
  • To treat my fellow club members and our guests with respect and courtesy.
  • To adhere to the guidelines and rules for all ECO Club’s education and recognition programs.
  • To maintain honest and highly ethical standards during the conduct of all ECO Club’s Environmental activities.
