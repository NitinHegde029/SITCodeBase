OBJECTIVES

  • To promote, setup or maintain facilities which import conservation education through outdoor education, camping and nature trails.
  • To promote, setup or maintain facilities to document the biodiversity of an area, study the habitat, conduct wildlife census etc.,
  • To establish, run, operate and maintain rehabilitation centres for injured wildlife, biodiversity parks for educating children.
  • Environment, Forest, Water and Wildlife Conservation Program.
  • To promote set up maintain facilities to promote academic rigor in youths, establish soft skill development centres, promote research and documentation.
  • To promote, set up or maintain facilities which manufacture eco friendly products, promote their usage to reduce the burden of waste management.
  • To establish facilities to promote eco tourism to expose youth and general public to appreciate the nature and natural resources across the globe.
  • To establish facilities to certify the standard operation procedures for the industries to minimize the pollution levels.
  • To work for the Socio-economic advancement of women, especially rural women, to make them independent and respectable citizens of the society.
  • To promote and work for advancement of Indian citizens of the society.
  • To acquire, purchase, take on lease or otherwise acquire lands to build buildings, tenements, sheds or other tenements to accomplish the object of this Trust.
  • To assist other trusts and charitable organizations, either financially or otherwise, who are engaged n such charitable objects are of or similar to that of this Trust.
  • To carry out all such acts, deeds and things which directly or indirectly promote or aid in the promotion of any of the objects mentioned herein above.
  • To promote and work for the Indian Culture and Literature.
  • To organize tree plantation programme.
  • To organize seeds collection programme and to donate the collected seeds to the Karnataka State Forest Nurseries.
  • To Organize environmental education programme
  • To promote young minds to work in the forest nurseries.
  • To develop Bio-Diversity Parks, Gomala lands, Lake sides etc.
  • Collaborate with country’s ‘Jalashakti Abhiyan’ Scheme and to implement AICTE prescribed activities and schemes such as ‘One Student One Tree’ , etc.
  • To Identify suitable dry lands and lakes collaboratively with the District administration and local bodies and to undertake a forestation works.
  • To promote Nature Photography among young minds by organizing Nature Photography Competition.
  • To organize eco tours.
  • To identify funding schemes of Government agencies and submission of project proposals to funding agencies to undertake environmental activities.
  • To rise funds from donors and under various schemes to execute environmental activities.
  • To promote the talented children of the members by giving scholarships and support those children’s financially for higher education if requested by the members.
  • Any other objects ancillary to the main set above.
  • Responding to the grievances of the members of the association and bringing their problems to the concerned authorities and assisting them with direct assistance from the government.
  • Enlighten public sentiments, such as literacy, equality, fraternity, coexistence and harmony.
  • Conducting cultural, social and educational development programs such as symposium, discussion, and children’s fair, workshop, lecture, vision, conference, webinars and so on.
  • Organizing free health screening, blood donation campaigns, psychological counselling, educational counselling, stress management programmes, encouraging people to use polio vaccine, etc.
  • To promote arts, literature and culture.
  • Agreement with other financial support cooperatives/organizations for the development of the association.
  • To organize friendly and educational tours for the association’s aspirants and aspirants in the educational, social and economic arena and to connect with other states.
  • To protect the public interests and organizing programmes that supports it.
  • To participate with the Government, local self, semi-government, research institutions, university, educational institutions and non-governmental organizations.
  • To utilize all donations, grants aid or any form offered by any individual/cooperative society/Government/Non-government/Society having same aims and objects trust for the achievement of the aims and objects of the association.
  • To carry out or cause to carry out all such benevolent things, acts and deeds which are incidental to any of the objects specified herein above.
  • To provide for promotion of welfare of general public and carry on any activity for general public utility in all possible ways.
  • To carry out the above objects either directly or indirectly or through others or jointly in any manner whatsoever.
  • To undertake all such lawful acts, deeds or things including sports and culture activities as are incidental and conductive to overall wellbeing of the members.
